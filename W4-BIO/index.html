ABOUT the Movie Transformers

Transformers first started out to be a cartoon in 1984 and lived a long life until now. Famous voice over for Optimus Prime PETER CULLEN still to this day does the voice for Optimus Prime. This makes anyone who watches the current dated movies feel like a kid again. In Transformers there are Two sides. The Autobots which is the good, then the Decepticons which is bad. The Autobots come to earth looking for the cube to help save Cybertron. Cybertron is a metal Planet where these robots come from. But they don't know where the cube is, and only ONE person on earth has the coordinates to the cube. His name is Sam Witwicky. Sam and his Girlfriend Mikaela Banes help the Autobots in there mission for saving earth and Cybertron. The Autobots are trying to beat the Decepticons to the key of the cube. Optimus Prime which is the Leader of the Autobots and Megatron the Leader of the Decepticons have a battle for the cube. In the End there is a Great outcome because the good guys ALWAYS WIN!
